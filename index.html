About Me

I'm a passionate software enthusiast and community leader with a deep love for gaming, technology, and building bridges between people.

For the past two years, I've served as a Community Manager for VALORANT at Riot Games, where I manage a community of 40,000+ Persian-speaking players across four international server regions — Istanbul, Dubai, Bahrain, and Frankfurt — all while operating in a country with no official Riot Games server.

I negotiated a unique arrangement with Riot, taking on the role with a performance-based compensation structure that reflects my deep commitment to the community and the game. This experience taught me to think creatively, operate under constraints, and lead with passion.
